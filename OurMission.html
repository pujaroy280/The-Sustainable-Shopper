Our Mission

Here at The Sustainable Shopper, we acknowledge that sustainability is closely tied to privilege, and people in marginalized communities are at a much higher risk of being affected by variable resource prices, climate unpredictability, and local environmental degradation.

Thousands of women and young children are exploited for cheap labour, the planet is being stripped of its resources, and these ruthless corporations don't seem to be stopping anytime soon.

The Sustainable Shopper hopes that in the future, sustainability will be accessible and affordable to all.
